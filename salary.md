# Salary Calculation Formula

## Daily Salary
Daily Salary = Monthly Salary ÷ 30

## Attendance Pay
Attendance Pay = Present Days × Daily Salary

## Overtime Pay
Overtime Pay = Overtime Hours × Overtime Rate

## Final Salary
Final Salary = Attendance Pay + Overtime Pay

## Productivity Score
Productivity Score = Attendance Percentage + Overtime Points - Leave Penalty

## Example

Monthly Salary = 30000

Present Days = 28

Overtime Hours = 10

Overtime Rate = 150

**Calculations:**

Daily Salary = 1000

Attendance Pay = 28000

Overtime Pay = 1500

Final Salary = 29500
